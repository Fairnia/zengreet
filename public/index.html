\' zen \:

A state of calm attentiveness in which one's actions are guided by intuition rather than by conscious effort

\' greet \:

To address with expression of kind wishes upon meeting
It's easy to feel stressed and disconnected amid the COVID-19 crisis
It's also easy to forget why we need to remain vigilant about staying safe
When you click "Start Connecting" you'll be paired with a random person, together you'll offer kind greetings:

May you be happy

May you be healthy

May you stay safe

You'll then be paired with another person and repeat. You're welcome to keep meeting new people for as long as you like!
By connecting with others and offering them sincere well wishes, we hope you'll feel more connected, hopeful, and motivated to stay safe

Tip: if you're having a tough time connecting through video, try using the Chrome browser.
